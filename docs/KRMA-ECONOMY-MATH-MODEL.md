## GM Economy

### Initial Allocation
- **Sign-on lump sum: ~4,000 KRMA** (covers 5 PCs + 10 NPCs + world content + 3-month buffer)
- Sign-on amount **rises over time** as metaverse matures (new GMs stay competitive)
- Estimated schedule (tunable):

| Year Joined | Sign-on Bonus |
|-------------|--------------|
| Y1 | 4,000 |
| Y2 | 4,200 |
| Y3 | 4,500 |
| Y5 | 5,500 |
| Y7 | 6,500 |
| Y10 | 8,000 |

### Monthly Allocation
- **Flat 400 KRMA/month** — same for all GMs regardless of tenure
- Funded from Terminal reserve
- No loyalty multiplier (prevents new GM disadvantage)

### Retention Bonuses (from Terminal)
- Milestone lump sums rewarding commitment:

| Milestone | Bonus |
|-----------|-------|
| 6 months | 1,000 |
| 1 year | 1,500 |
| 2 years | 2,500 |
| 3 years | 3,000 |
| 5 years | 5,000 |

### GM Wallet Growth Sources
1. Monthly subscription allocation (Terminal → GM)
2. GRO.vine gifts from God-heads (God-head wallet → character TKV → grows GM wallet)
3. Creative royalties (other GMs deploy their blueprints)
4. Nectar breakdown tax (~10%, when players convert nectars to frequency)

## God-head Economy (Pillar Reserves → Campaigns)

### Funding Model
- 25B total across three pillar reserves
- God-heads funded via seasonal events + council allocations
- 4 seasons/year, distribution rate scales with demand

### GRO.vine Gift Demand

### God-head KRMA Recycling (Reduces Net Drain)
- **Blossoms return** when expired (~30% of gifts are blossoms? → ~30% recycled)
- **Thorn collection on death** — Lady Death settles from her wallet, God-heads reclaim
- **Creative royalties** flow between God-heads (internal circulation)
- Estimated net drain: **~60-70% of gross** after recycling

### Net Pillar Reserve Drain
- Gross 10-year spend: ~10-12B
- Net after recycling: ~6-8B
- Pillar reserves: 25B
- **Remaining after 10 years: ~17-19B (68-76%)**

**Verdict: Pillar reserves are very healthy.** God-heads are well-funded for a 10-year arc and beyond. The seasonal allocation rate can increase as the player base grows.

## Lady Death's Economy

### Income
- **Frequency from all deaths** — entire frequency pool of every dying character
- Settlement of thorn liens (she pays God-heads, keeps the remainder)

### Death Rate Estimates

Assumptions:
- ~20% annual character mortality (1 death per 5 characters per year)
- Average frequency at death varies by character age:
  - Young characters (< 1 year): ~60 frequency
  - Mid characters (1-2 years): ~150 frequency
  - Veteran characters (3+ years): ~300 frequency
  - Weighted average: ~120 frequency at death

## Burn Sink

- 5B global cap
- Burn rate depends entirely on player behavior
- At D&D scale, if 0.1% of all frequency transactions are burns:
  - Total frequency in system by Y10: ~50-60B KRMA in character pools
  - 0.1% burn rate: ~50-60M/year by Y10
  - Time to reach 5B cap: **50-80 years** (well beyond 10-year arc)
- Even at 1% burn rate: cap reached in ~8-10 years
- **Burn is a slow sink** — reveals create pressure but actual depletion is gradual
- Reveal timing: organic, decided narratively by Admin

## Validation Checks

### Can a starting GM run a campaign?
- Lump sum: 4,000 KRMA
- 5 PCs at ~200 each: 1,000
- 10 NPCs at ~100 each: 1,000
- World content (items, locations, materials): ~500
- Starting allocated: 2,500 / 4,000 capacity
- Headroom: 1,500 (37.5%) — enough for 3 months of growth before monthly kicks in
- **YES ✓**

### Can characters reach god-tier?
- Starting: 200 KRMA
- Growth rate: ~60-75/month from GRO.vines
- Time to 3,000: (3,000 - 200) / 67.5 = ~41 months (~3.4 years)
- **YES ✓** — achievable in 3-4 years of consistent play

### Does the GM wallet keep up with character growth?
- 5 PCs growing at ~65/month = 325/month campaign growth from GRO.vines
- GM monthly allocation: 400/month
- Total monthly wallet growth: 725/month
- Of which ~325 is GRO.vine (already allocated to characters), 400 is unallocated
- **YES ✓** — GM always has building room

### Can Lady Death afford thorn settlements?
- Annual income by Y5: ~72M
- Annual thorn settlement by Y5: ~10-15M
- **YES ✓** — Lady Death is solvent with massive surplus for her own agency

### Does 100B conservation hold?
- All KRMA is either in reserves, wallets, characters, spirit packages, or burned
- No minting. Thorns are liens (no duplication). Deaths redistribute, don't destroy.
- Burn is the only true sink, capped at 5B.
- **YES ✓**
