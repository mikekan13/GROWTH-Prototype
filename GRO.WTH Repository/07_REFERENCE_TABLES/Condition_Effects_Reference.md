# Condition Effects Reference

Comprehensive reference for all status conditions, their effects, and recovery methods in GROWTH.

## Raw Magic Monkey Paws

Effects organized by **spell strength** and **success level** for [[Casting_Methods|Wild Casting]].

### Spell Strength Classifications

| Strength Level | DR Range | Monkey Paw Severity |
|---|---|---|
| **Insignificant** | 0-9 | Trivial inconveniences |
| **Weak** | 10-19 | Minor complications |
| **Moderate** | 20-39 | Noticeable problems |
| **Strong** | 40-59 | Serious consequences |
| **Powerful** | 60-79 | Severe complications |
| **Epic** | 80-89 | Extreme consequences |
| **Godly** | 90+ | Catastrophic effects |

### Success Level Modifiers

**Perfect Success (5+ over DR):** No monkey paw  
**Success (0-4 over DR):** Trivial monkey paw possible  
**Failure (-4 to -1):** Monkey paw imposed  
**Critical Failure (-5 or worse):** Severe monkey paw

## Physical Conditions

### Strain and Fatigue
**Strain Accumulation:** Physical and mental exhaustion from overexertion  
**Recovery Methods:**
- **Sleep:** Heals at natural healing rate
- **Food:** Removes 2 points of strain
- **Sex:** Removes 5 points of strain  
- **Recreation:** Removes 1-3 points depending on activity
- **Medical Treatment:** Professional care accelerates recovery

### Injury Conditions
**Light Wounds:** -1 to physical actions, heals naturally  
**Moderate Wounds:** -2 to affected actions, requires medical attention  
**Severe Wounds:** -3 to all actions, requires magical or advanced medical care  
**Critical Wounds:** Character incapacitated, immediate treatment required

## Hit Location Conditions

### Head & Neck Injuries
**Effect:** Lose **all offensive actions** per turn  
**Duration:** Until medical treatment or magical healing  
**Recovery:** Requires skilled medical intervention  
**Complications:** Risk of permanent damage if untreated

### Torso Injuries  
**Effect:** Lose **one chosen action** per turn  
**Duration:** Until medical treatment or magical healing  
**Recovery:** Standard medical care sufficient  
**Strategic Impact:** Significantly reduces combat effectiveness

### Limb Injuries
**Arm Damage:**
- Cannot use two-handed weapons
- Cannot perform actions requiring both hands  
- -2 to climbing and grappling

**Leg Damage:**
- Movement speed reduced by half
- -2 to dodge attempts
- -3 to running and jumping

## Magical Conditions

### Magical Resistance States
**Magic Immunity:** Complete protection against specific schools  
**Magic Resistance:** Reduced effectiveness of magical attacks  
**Magic Vulnerability:** Increased damage from magical sources  
**Magic Disruption:** Inability to cast or maintain spells

### Enchantment Effects
**Charm:** Targets view caster as trusted friend  
**Domination:** Complete mental control over target  
**Confusion:** Random actions, cannot act coherently  
**Fear:** Must flee from source, -3 to all actions while affected

## Environmental Conditions

### Temperature Extremes
**Hypothermia:** Gradual loss of actions, eventual unconsciousness  
**Heat Exhaustion:** Increased strain accumulation, dehydration  
**Frostbite:** Permanent damage to extremities  
**Burns:** Ongoing damage and scarring

### Atmospheric Conditions  
**Suffocation:** Progressive damage until breathing restored  
**Poison Gas:** Varied effects depending on toxin type  
**High Altitude:** Reduced stamina and increased strain  
**Underwater:** Drowning risk and movement penalties

## Disease and Poison

### Disease Progression
**Incubation:** No symptoms, contagious period begins  
**Onset:** Initial symptoms appear, penalties begin  
**Peak:** Full disease effects, maximum penalties  
**Recovery:** Gradual improvement with treatment  
**Immunity:** Resistance to future infection

### Poison Types
**Paralytic:** Gradual loss of actions, eventual immobilization  
**Hallucinogenic:** Perception penalties, confusion effects  
**Lethal:** Progressive damage until treatment or death  
**Psychoactive:** Mental condition changes, personality alterations

## Social Conditions

### Reputation Effects
**Fame:** Positive recognition bonuses in social situations  
**Infamy:** Negative reaction penalties, legal complications  
**Anonymity:** No social recognition, both positive and negative  
**Notoriety:** Mixed reactions based on observer alignment

### Economic Conditions
**Wealth:** Access to resources and services  
**Debt:** Ongoing obligations and penalties  
**Poverty:** Limited options and increased difficulties  
**Prosperity:** Enhanced opportunities and social access

## Recovery Methods

### Natural Healing
**Rest:** Standard recovery for minor conditions  
**Time:** Many conditions resolve naturally given sufficient time  
**Constitution:** Higher Constitution speeds natural recovery  
**Environment:** Clean, safe conditions improve recovery rate

### Medical Treatment
**First Aid:** Immediate stabilization and minor treatment  
**Professional Medicine:** Trained healer reduces recovery time  
**Surgery:** Complex procedures for severe conditions  
**Rehabilitation:** Extended treatment for permanent injuries

### Magical Healing
**Restoration Magic:** Immediate condition removal  
**Healing Potions:** Gradual condition improvement  
**Divine Intervention:** Miraculous healing from deities  
**Magical Items:** Ongoing condition resistance or immunity
