# Roots Examples

Roots represent formative childhood experiences and upbringing that shape a character's early development, granting additional skills and attribute improvements.

## Warrior's Childhood Root

**Background:** Character grew up in a harsh, war-torn environment that fostered toughness and resilience.

**Benefits:**
- **Skills:** Survival (Level 3), Weapon Training (Level 2)
- **Attributes:** +3 Constitution, +2 Clout
- **Theme:** Military upbringing, conflict exposure, survival training

### Character Impact
This Root reflects a childhood shaped by warfare and hardship, resulting in:
- Enhanced physical durability and strength
- Practical survival skills from necessity
- Combat training from early age
- Mental resilience from trauma exposure

## Root Selection Guidance

Roots should reflect:
- **Formative Years:** Ages roughly 6-16 depending on species
- **Environmental Factors:** Location, culture, family situation
- **Defining Experiences:** Major events that shaped personality
- **Skill Development:** Natural talents cultivated in youth
- **Attribute Growth:** Physical and mental development patterns

### Integration with Background
Players can:
1. **Story First:** Create backstory, then select matching Root
2. **Mechanics First:** Choose Root, then build story around it  
3. **Collaborative:** Work with GM to design custom Roots
