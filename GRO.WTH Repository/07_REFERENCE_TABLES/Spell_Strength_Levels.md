# Spell Strength Levels Reference

Quick reference for magic power classifications and their corresponding Difficulty Ratings.

## Level Classifications

| Level | Name | DR Range | Authority Level | Description |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | Insignificant | 1-9 | GM Discretion | Minor magical effects |
| **2** | Weak | 10-19 | GM Discretion | Basic magical abilities |
| **3** | Empowered | 20-29 | GM Discretion | Noticeable magical power |
| **4** | Strong | 30-39 | GM Discretion | Significant magical effects |
| **5** | Mighty | 40-49 | GM Discretion | Major magical phenomena |
| **6** | Heroic | 50-59 | Terminal Oversight | Legendary magical power |
| **7** | Legendary | 60-69 | Terminal Oversight | Epic magical effects |
| **8** | Mythical | 70-79 | Terminal Oversight | Reality-bending magic |
| **9** | Titanic | 80-89 | Terminal Oversight | World-shaking power |
| **10** | Godly | 90+ | Terminal Oversight | Divine-level reality alteration |

## Authority Thresholds

### GM Full Authority (Levels 1-5, DR 1-49)
- Complete discretion over DR assignment and modifications
- Can freely adjust effects, requirements, and outcomes
- Standard magical effects for typical gameplay

### Terminal Oversight (Levels 6-10, DR 50+)
- DR assignments become largely non-negotiable
- [[LLM_Assisted_Magic_Creation|System enforces]] consistency with precedents
- High-level effects may impact broader game universe
- Level 8+ magic affects meta-narrative across campaigns

## Power Scaling Examples

### Lower Levels (1-5)
**Level 1 - Light Candle:** DR 5, simple utility magic  
**Level 3 - Heal Wounds:** DR 25, significant but routine healing  
**Level 5 - Teleport Group:** DR 45, major tactical advantage

### Higher Levels (6-10)
**Level 6 - Raise Dead:** DR 55, legendary life restoration  
**Level 8 - Alter Timeline:** DR 75, reality-bending temporal magic  
**Level 10 - Reshape Continent:** DR 95, divine geographical alteration

## Mana Requirements

### Standard Casting Limits
**Maximum Natural Roll:** d20 + d20 = 40 maximum without mana  
**Level 1-4 Magic:** Often achievable with skill alone  
**Level 5+ Magic:** Requires [[Mana_System|mana enhancement]] for reasonable success chances  
**Level 8+ Magic:** Massive mana investment essential for success

### Strategic Implications
**Resource Planning:** Higher levels require significant [[KRMA_System|karmic investment]]  
**Campaign Impact:** Level 6+ magic becomes major story events  
**Meta-Consequences:** Level 8+ magic may permanently alter game universe
