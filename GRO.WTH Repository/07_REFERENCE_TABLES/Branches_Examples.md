# Branches Examples

Branches represent significant adventures, jobs, training, or experiences that occurred during late adolescence and adulthood, providing additional skills, attributes, and specialized knowledge.

## Mercenary Branch

**Background:** Character spent years working as a hired warrior, learning tactical combat and survival across various environments.

**Benefits:**
- **Skills:** Tactics (Level 2), Hand-to-Hand Combat (Level 3)
- **Attributes:** +2 Clout, +2 Constitution
- **Theme:** Professional warfare, tactical thinking, combat expertise

### Character Impact
This Branch reflects years of professional combat experience:
- Advanced combat techniques and weapon mastery
- Tactical understanding of battlefield dynamics  
- Enhanced physical capabilities from constant training
- Professional warrior mindset and discipline

## Branch Selection Criteria

Branches represent:
- **Adult Experiences:** Post-adolescent career or major life events
- **Professional Training:** Formal education, apprenticeships, military service
- **Major Adventures:** Significant journeys or quests undertaken
- **Life-Changing Events:** Experiences that fundamentally altered the character
- **Specialized Knowledge:** Expert-level skills in specific areas

### Development Timeline
- **Age Requirements:** Branches add age based on duration of experience
- **Multiple Branches:** Characters may have several Branches representing different periods
- **Karmic Costs:** More powerful Branches require higher Karmic Level investment
- **Campaign Integration:** Branches should align with campaign setting and timeline

### Branch Categories
- **Professional:** Military service, guild membership, formal careers
- **Adventurous:** Exploration, treasure hunting, dangerous expeditions  
- **Academic:** Scholarly pursuit, magical training, research
- **Social:** Noble upbringing, criminal enterprises, religious service
