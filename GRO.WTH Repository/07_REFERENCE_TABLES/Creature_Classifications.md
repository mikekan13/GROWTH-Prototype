# Creature Classifications

GROWTH uses a **comprehensive creature rating system** from basic animals to cosmic entities, providing complete stat frameworks for encounters.

## Basic Creature Categories (DR 2-5)

| Creature Type | DR | HP | Body | Armor | Attack | Damage | APT | KV |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Small Animal** | 2 | 3 | 2 | 0 | 4 | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| **Medium Animal** | 3 | 5 | 3 | 1 | 5 | 2 | 2 | 2 |
| **Large Animal** | 4 | 8 | 4 | 1 | 6 | 3 | 2 | 3 |
| **Apex Predator** | 5 | 12 | 5 | 2 | 7 | 4 | 3 | 5 |

## Divine Classifications (DR 30+)

### Demi-Gods (DR 30-40)
**Description:** Lesser divine beings with significant but limited power  
**Examples:** Saints, ascended heroes, minor deities  
**Capabilities:** Regional influence, miraculous abilities  
**Mortality:** Can be killed but very difficult

### Lesser Gods (DR 50-60)  
**Description:** True deities with substantial divine power  
**Examples:** Domain gods, pantheon members  
**Capabilities:** Major reality alteration within domains  
**Mortality:** Extremely difficult to kill, requires divine weapons

### Greater Gods (DR 70-90)
**Description:** Major deities with vast cosmic influence  
**Examples:** Creator gods, pantheon leaders  
**Capabilities:** Universal laws, reality creation  
**Mortality:** Nearly impossible to kill permanently

### Cosmic Entities (DR 100-150)
**Description:** Beings that transcend normal divine categories  
**Examples:** Primordial forces, abstract concepts made manifest  
**Capabilities:** Fundamental reality alteration  
**Mortality:** Death would affect universal structure

### Ultimate Entities (DR 200+)

#### Ein Sof (DR 200)
**Description:** The ultimate divine principle  
**Nature:** Source of all existence  
**Capabilities:** Absolute reality control  
**Mortality:** Death would end existence itself

## Stat Block Components

### Standard Attributes
**DR:** Difficulty Rating for actions against the creature  
**HP:** Hit Points or damage capacity  
**Body:** Physical resilience and strength  
**Armor:** Natural or worn protection  
**Attack:** Combat capability rating  
**Damage:** Harm potential per successful attack  
**APT:** [[Turn_Structure_and_Action_Economy|Actions Per Turn]] in combat  
**KV:** [[KRMA_System|Karma Value]] for defeat rewards

### Special Abilities
**Magical Resistance:** Immunity or resistance to specific [[Three_Pillars_Overview|magic types]]  
**Supernatural Senses:** Enhanced perception abilities  
**Flight/Teleportation:** Movement beyond normal limitations  
**Regeneration:** Self-healing capabilities  
**Environmental Immunity:** Resistance to natural hazards

## Encounter Scaling

### Party Level Matching
**Equal Challenge:** Creature DR approximately equals party average  
**Easy Encounter:** Creature DR 2-3 points below party average  
**Hard Encounter:** Creature DR 2-3 points above party average  
**Boss Encounter:** Creature DR 5+ points above party average

### Group Encounters
**Multiple Weak:** Several creatures 3-5 DR below party level  
**Mixed Groups:** Combination of different CR creatures  
**Elite with Minions:** One strong creature with several weak supporters
