# Starting Skills Module

This module provides **skill name examples** to inspire character creation. These are **suggestions only** - players can create any skill names that fit their character concept. Skills use freeform naming with no required categories.

**Important:** These examples serve as inspiration, not limitations. Players should create skill names that reflect their character's unique background and specialization.

## ATHLETICS
*Physical capabilities and bodily coordination*

- **Swim** - Water navigation and aquatic survival
- **Sneak** - Stealth movement and concealment  
- **Manual Dexterity** - Fine motor control and precision tasks
- **Climb** - Vertical movement and rope work
- **Acrobatics** - Balance, tumbling, and aerial maneuvers

## SOCIAL SKILLS  
*Interpersonal interaction and influence*

- **Seduction** - Physical attraction and romantic influence
- **Intimidation** - Fear-based coercion and threat
- **Negotiation** - Bargaining and diplomatic resolution
- **Deception** - Lying and misdirection  
- **Leadership** - Group coordination and inspiration

## MARTIAL ARTS
*Combat proficiencies organized by weapon type*

### Weapon Categories
- **Swords** - Blade combat specialization
- **Polearms** - Long-hafted weapon combat
- **Bows** - Ranged projectile weapons
- **Firearms** - Gunpowder and advanced projectile weapons  
- **Unarmed** - Hand-to-hand combat techniques
- **Shields** - Defensive weapon techniques
- **Exotic Weapons** - Specialized or cultural weapons

## SCIENCES
*Systematic knowledge and research skills*

### Natural Sciences
- **Biology** - Living organism study
- **Chemistry** - Material composition and reaction
- **Physics** - Natural law understanding
- **Geology** - Earth science and minerals

### Applied Sciences  
- **Engineering** - Construction and mechanical design
- **Medicine** - Healing and biological intervention
- **Alchemy** - Magical chemistry and transmutation
- **Architecture** - Building design and construction

### Social Sciences
- **Psychology** - Mind and behavior study
- **History** - Past events and cultural knowledge
- **Economics** - Resource distribution and trade
- **Politics** - Governance and power structures

## ARTS
*Creative expression and artistic skills*

### Performing Arts
- **Music** - Instrumental and vocal performance
- **Dance** - Choreographed movement expression  
- **Theater** - Acting and dramatic performance
- **Oratory** - Public speaking and presentation

### Visual Arts
- **Painting** - Two-dimensional visual creation
- **Sculpture** - Three-dimensional artistic creation
- **Drawing** - Sketching and illustration
- **Crafting** - Decorative object creation

## PERCEPTION
*Information gathering and awareness skills*

- **Social Insight** - Reading people and motivations
- **Environmental Awareness** - Detecting physical changes and threats
- **ESP (Extrasensory Perception)** - Supernatural information gathering

## Skill Level Integration

All skills use the [[Skill_Level_Progression]] system:
- **Level 0:** Untrained (no roll permitted)
- **Level 1-3:** Flat Bonus (+1, +2, +3)
- **Level 4-5:** Decoder (d4)
- **Level 6-7:** Competent (d6)
- **Level 8-9:** Professional (d8)
- **Level 10-20:** Master to Godlike (d12-d20)

## Skill Synergy

**Cross-Category Combinations:** Skills from different categories can combine for enhanced effects  
**Specialization Bonuses:** Related skills may provide bonuses to each other  
**Professional Integration:** Real-world professions typically combine multiple skills

### Synergy Examples
**Medicine + Alchemy:** Enhanced healing capabilities  
**Leadership + Intimidation:** Command presence in combat  
**Sneak + Environmental Awareness:** Superior infiltration ability
