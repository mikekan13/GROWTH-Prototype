# Weapon Categories Reference

## Weapon Classification System

Based on the established [[Weapon_System|weapon system]], weapons are classified by several factors:

### Size Categories
- **Light Weapons:** One-handed, fast to use
- **Medium Weapons:** One-handed, balanced
- **Heavy Weapons:** Two-handed, powerful
- **Ranged Weapons:** Projectile-based

### Combat Skills Required
Each weapon type requires appropriate [[Complete_Skill_List|Martial Arts]] specialization:
- **Sword Fighting:** All blade weapons
- **Axe Fighting:** All axe weapons  
- **Mace Fighting:** All blunt weapons
- **Polearm Fighting:** Long-hafted weapons
- **Archery:** Bow weapons
- **Crossbow:** Crossbow weapons
- **Thrown Weapons:** Projectile weapons
- **Unarmed Combat:** Fists, improvised weapons

### Material Categories
Weapons can be made from different [[Material_System|materials]]:
- **Stone:** Primitive materials
- **Bone:** Organic materials
- **Wood:** Organic, flexible
- **Iron:** Standard metal
- **Steel:** Superior metal
- **Magical Materials:** Enhanced properties

### Damage Types
Based on [[Damage_Types_and_Effects|damage system]] using format `P:S:H/D\C:B:E`:
- **Piercing (P):** Points and thrusts (arrows, spears, stabs)
- **Slashing (S):** Blade weapons, cutting damage (swords, axes)
- **Bashing (B):** Impact weapons, crushing damage (clubs, hammers)
- **Heat (H):** Fire/thermal weapons
- **Decay (D):** Corrosive weapons
- **Cold (C):** Ice/freezing weapons
- **Energy (E):** Electrical/lightning weapons

## Combat Integration

### Action Requirements
All weapon attacks require one [[Turn_Structure_and_Action_Economy|Body action]] (or appropriate pillar action based on weapon type).

### Multiple Attacks
Higher [[Skill_Level_Progression|skill levels]] grant additional attacks:
- Levels 1-5: 1 attack per action
- Levels 6-10: 2 attacks per action  
- Levels 11-15: 3 attacks per action
- Levels 16-20: 4 attacks per action

### Equipment Conditions
All weapons are subject to the [[Equipment_Conditions|condition degradation system]] and require maintenance.

## Integration with Other Systems

### Wealth Level
Weapon acquisition is limited by character [[Wealth_Level_System|Wealth Level]] and availability.

### Technology Level  
Weapon types are restricted by campaign [[Technology_Level_System|Technology Level]]:
- **Levels 1-2:** Stone, bone, basic metal weapons
- **Levels 3-4:** Advanced metallurgy, gunpowder weapons
- **Levels 5+:** Modern and advanced weapons

### Encumbrance
All weapons contribute to [[Inventory_and_Encumbrance_System|carrying capacity]] based on size and material.
