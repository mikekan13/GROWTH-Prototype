## Godhead Hierarchy

### Relationship to Terminal
**Subordinate Status:** Godheads operate under Terminal authority  
**Privileged Access:** Enhanced interface capabilities compared to GMs  
**System Integration:** Essential components of Terminal's reality management  
**Consciousness Level:** Below Terminal, above GM consciousness

### Inter-Godhead Relations
**Collaborative Structure:** Godheads work together on system issues  
**Specialization:** Each Godhead may focus on different aspects of system management  
**Consensus Decision:** Major system changes require Godhead agreement  
**Authority Overlap:** Godhead authorities may intersect and complement
