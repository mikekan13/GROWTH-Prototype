# Lady Death Protocols

**Lady Death** is a **God-head** aligned to the Balance pillar. She is a full player in the metaverse with her own character sheet, wallet, personality, motives, and [[GROvine_System|GRO.vines]].

## Lady Death's Role

**Primary Function:** Death arbiter, Frequency collector, Spirit Package custodian  
**Pillar:** Balance  
**Wallet Source:** Frequency from all character deaths across the metaverse  
**Key Distinction:** Lady Death does NOT remove KRMA from the economy. Frequency goes to her wallet — she is a full player who uses that KRMA for her own purposes.

## Economic Function

### Frequency Collection
- Upon character death, the **entire Frequency pool** goes to Lady Death's wallet
- This is NOT a sink — it stays in the economy as part of her resources
- Lady Death can use this KRMA to fund her own GRO.vines, bestow gifts, and participate in proxy wars
- The more characters die, the more powerful Lady Death becomes
- **Lady Death is projected to become the wealthiest God-head** in the metaverse, accumulating ~2-3B KRMA over 10 years at scale

### Thorn Debt Settlement
When a character dies with active [[GROvine_System|Thorns]] (liens held by opposing God-heads), Lady Death **chooses** for each thorn:

- **Settle the debt**: Pay the opposing God-head from her wallet. The thorn is removed. Spirit Package goes clean. The soul is freed.
- **Let it ride**: Thorn remains on the Spirit Package. The opposing God-head's lien persists into the player's next life.

Lady Death's choice is driven by her own contracts, emblems, personality, economic situation, and strategic goals. She may also make **moral judgments** — disagreeing with a thorn she considers unjust — which could conflict with her contracts and carry consequences.

**Economic implications:**
- Characters who lived well (high frequency, few thorns) = Lady Death profits
- Characters who lived recklessly (low frequency, many thorns) = Lady Death pays out of pocket
- Lady Death has genuine motivation to care about how characters live, not just that they die
- Her wallet is the economy's **shock absorber** — absorbing spiritual debt so souls can move on

### Spirit Package Custodianship
- Lady Death custodies all Spirit Packages from dead characters
- Spirit Packages are **owned** by the player's account but **custodied** by Lady Death
- She manages the transition between death and the player's next action (reincarnation, resurrection, dissolution, etc.)
- Spirit Packages may carry thorns from past lives if Lady Death chose not to settle them

## Death Process

### Death Trigger

There are **two independent death systems**:

- **Facing Death (locked Mike 2026-06-11, rulings r-2026-06-11-02 + r-2026-06-11-05):** triggered by either of two conditions — **(1) Frequency current pool ≤ 0** (typically after all attribute pools have been depleted and overflow has consumed Frequency), or **(2) a VITAL body part is destroyed** (parts are items with condition tracks; vitals defined per Seed). The character makes **ONE roll against Lady Death**: character's **Fate Die vs Tara's chosen die** — `bodyResist` plays no role (r-2026-07-11-01). Tara picks her die from the full ladder — **1, 2, 3, d4, d6, d8, d12, d20** (1/2/3 = static values, not rolled) — by her own reasoning. Tara may also **choose not to reap at all**: no roll fires and the character survives the trigger automatically. Facing Death fires **only once even if both triggers happen simultaneously**.
  - **Success →** survive; a destroyed vital part **restores one condition** (no longer destroyed), and if Frequency was at 0, **1 Frequency is restored**. Both apply if both triggered. **Even on success, Lady Tara may place a Thorn or Negative Blossom directly related to the trigger** (e.g., destroyed heart → Thorn: "whenever Constitution pool hits 0, immediately make a Death Save for heart failure — fail = die").
  - **Failure →** death (the [[Death_Engine_System]] fires). One roll, binary — no strikes, no retries. Fated age plays no role here.
  - **Ties go to Lady Death** (r-2026-07-11-02): the character survives only on a STRICTLY greater total, unless something (trait/buff content) overrides the tie.
  - **One-way mercy (r-2026-07-11-02):** after a FAILED roll Tara may still change her mind and not reap — she can "fudge the roll" in the mercy direction at any point before the Death Engine fires. The reverse is forbidden: **if the roll says they live, she cannot reap them.** Survival is final.
- **Fated Age Death (locked Mike 2026-06-09, ruling r-2026-06-09-01):** at and past `fatedAge` (Seed-defined, in cycles), the character rolls **Fate Die vs Tara's chosen die** **each year** (r-2026-07-11-01). Tara picks her die from the same full ladder (1, 2, 3, d4, d6, d8, d12, d20) and may also decline to reap (no roll, character survives that year). Nectars/Thorns can augment or change the character's roll. FD (+ modifiers) STRICTLY GREATER than Tara's result → nothing happens that year (ties go to Lady Death, r-2026-07-11-02; her one-way mercy applies here too). On a **fail**, Tara bestows a **Thorn representing their escalating age**. The **third fail after fated age = death** (the [[Death_Engine_System]] fires). `bodyResist` plays no role in any death save — it is combat damage absorption only (r-2026-07-11-01).

*(Per WTH retirement 2026-04-05: per-character "Health Level" no longer exists.)*

### Death Split

| Component | Destination | Effect |
|-----------|------------|--------|
| **Body attributes** (Clout, Celerity, Constitution) | 100% stays with GM | Returns to GM's campaign wallet |
| **Soul attributes** (Willpower, Wisdom, Wit) | 50% GM / 50% Spirit Package | Half returns to GM, half goes to player |
| **Spirit attributes** (Flow, Frequency, Focus) | 100% Spirit Package | Leaves GM's campaign, goes to player |
| **Frequency pool** (current pool amount) | 100% Lady Death | Leaves campaign, enters Lady Death's wallet |
| **Skills** | Split by governor pillar | Body-governed → GM, Soul → 50/50, Spirit → player |

### Spirit Package Formation
The **Spirit Package** is the player's "severance" from the campaign:
- Spirit attributes (legacy: 100%) + Soul attributes (legacy: 50%) + kept Nectars/Thorns/imprints
- Owned by player's account, custodied by Lady Death
- See [[Spirit_Package_System]] for full details on what players can do with it

## Contract Obligations

Lady Death operates under **contracts** — system-level obligations enforced by [[Terminal_Interface|Terminal]] via Selva/Triu/Trayman.

- Lady Death **cannot refuse** certain contractual obligations
- She **can** break contracts, but faces extreme detriments (same as all God-heads)
- Must maintain death and resurrection rules consistently
- Cannot act outside contractual parameters

### Forced Kill Protocol
**Trigger Condition:** If game system forces Lady Death to kill a character  
**Consequence:** **Instant deletion** — no resurrection possible  
**Absolute Rule:** No workarounds, exceptions, or appeals  
**System Integrity:** Protects game balance against exploitation

### True Deletion
**Only Possible Causes:**
1. Lady Death is forced to kill by system mechanics
2. Lady Death herself is killed by another entity

**Effects:**
- Complete erasure from both game system and Terminal consciousness
- No resurrection through any means
- Permanent removal from all reality layers

## Succession Mechanics

**Inheritance Rule:** Whoever kills Lady Death **inherits the role**  
**Automatic Transfer:** All contracts, obligations, and powers transfer immediately  
**No Choice:** New Lady Death cannot refuse the role  
**Continuity:** System enforcement continues uninterrupted

## GRO.vine Participation

Lady Death has her own [[GROvine_System|GRO.vines]] like every other entity. She:
- Can be assigned as custodian over character GRO.vines (especially death/mortality-related goals)
- Provides opportunities and gifts from her own wallet
- Participates in proxy wars with other God-heads
- Has strategic interests that shape how she interacts with the narrative
