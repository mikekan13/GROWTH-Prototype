# Harvests System

**Harvests** represent **significant milestones** between major campaign events, providing characters opportunities for **reflection, development, and preparation** for future challenges.

## Core Concept

**Definition:** Bookends to sagas within a larger story  
**Purpose:** Allow characters to reflect on journey and prepare for what lies ahead  
**Frequency:** Variable - campaigns may have few key Harvests, many small ones, or none for one-shots  
**Pattern Recognition:** Seeds (1), Roots (2), Branches (3), Harvests (4) - the natural character development sequence

## Harvest Mechanics

### GM-Initiated Process
**Trigger:** GM essentially asks "What were you doing during this stretch of time?"  
**Player Response:** Players narrate their characters' activities and development  
**Narrative Approach:** Mirrors character creation process through storytelling  
**Collaborative:** Contract between player and GM recognizing shared storytelling effort

### Character Development Focus
**Time Investment:** Characters dedicate extended periods to specific growth activities  
**Narrative Control:** Players direct their character's off-screen development  
**Organic Growth:** Development emerges from character choices rather than arbitrary advancement  
**Story Integration:** Harvest activities become part of campaign history

## GM Role in Harvest Implementation

### Defining and Awarding Harvests
**Assessment:** Based on players' narratives and campaign progression  
**Customization:** GM uses or creates appropriate Harvests for specific situations  
**Flexibility:** Duration can vary from short intervals to significant periods  
**Player Desires:** GM considers what players want for their characters

### Harvest Reward Examples

#### Training Montage
**Activity:** Character spends year training rigorously (like Rocky)  
**Reward:** +1 Level to all [[Three_Pillar_Attributes|Body Attributes]]  
**Time Cost:** Character ages appropriately  
**Narrative:** Physical transformation through dedicated effort

#### Study and Research  
**Activity:** Character dedicates time to studying ancient tomes and magical texts  
**Reward:** New powerful [[Casting_Methods|Woven Spell]] or increased [[Three_Pillar_Attributes|Focus]] and [[Three_Pillar_Attributes|Wisdom]]  
**Time Cost:** Extended period of scholarly pursuit  
**Narrative:** Intellectual and magical growth

#### Crafting and Creation
**Activity:** Character focuses on crafting weapons and armor  
**Reward:** Equipment enhancements or new [[Complete_Skill_List|crafting skills]]  
**Time Cost:** Months or years of dedicated work  
**Narrative:** Mastery of creation and construction

### Reward Categories
- **Attribute Increases:** Direct statistical improvements
- **Skill Development:** New skills or skill level increases  
- **Equipment:** Enhanced or new items
- **[[KRMA_Costs_Table|Wealth]]:** Improved financial status
- **Tech Levels:** Access to better technology
- **[[Nectars_and_Thorns_System|Nectars]]:** New beneficial traits from experiences

## Time Investment Requirements

### Dual Time Cost
**In-Game:** Characters age and spend significant periods on development  
**Real-World:** GMs require planning time to implement meaningful Harvests  
**Balance:** GM must manage in-game time passage with real-world preparation schedule

### Mechanical Aging
**Consequence:** Character advancement through Harvests costs time from lifespan
**Integration:** Works with [[Health_Level_System|Fated Age]] mechanics
**Realistic Development:** Reflects that mastery requires time investment  
**Strategic Choice:** Players must balance immediate power vs long-term survival

## Terminal Integration

### GM Assistance
**Support:** [[Terminal_Interface|Terminal]] provides tools and resources for Harvest management  
**Implementation:** Helps GMs craft meaningful Harvests aligned with player desires  
**Balance:** Assists with mechanical balance and narrative consistency  
**Efficiency:** Reduces GM workload for complex Harvest design

### Narrative Consistency
**Continuity:** Ensures Harvest outcomes align with campaign tone and style  
**Integration:** Helps weave Harvest results into ongoing campaign narrative  
**Player Agency:** Maintains player control while providing GM tools

## Harvest Flexibility

### Customization Potential
**Tailored Rewards:** Each Harvest can be unique to character and situation  
**Player Collaboration:** GM and players work together to create meaningful development  
**Narrative Fit:** Rewards emerge from character activities and choices  
**Campaign Integration:** Harvests serve broader story and character arcs

### Strategic Timing
**Story Breaks:** Natural points between major campaign arcs  
**Character Needs:** When characters require development or reflection time  
**Campaign Pacing:** Provides breathing room between intense adventures  
**Long-term Planning:** Allows setup for future campaign elements
