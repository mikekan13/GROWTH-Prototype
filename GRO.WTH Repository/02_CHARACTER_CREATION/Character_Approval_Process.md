# Character Approval Process

Once a character sheet is drafted through the Seeds, Roots, and Branches selection process, the approval system ensures balance, campaign integration, and authentication through the EŶ**∃**tehrNET.

## Submission Phase

### Preparing for Approval
1. **Complete Character Sheet:** Ensure all sections are filled out:
   - Background story
   - All attributes and pools
   - Skills and specializations  
   - Starting inventory and equipment
   - Nectars and Thorns
   - Age and [[KRMA_System|KRMA/KV]] calculations

2. **Review Requirements:** Verify character meets:
   - Campaign-specific restrictions
   - Power level guidelines
   - Setting integration requirements
   - GM's thematic preferences

### Submit to GM
- **GROWTH Software Integration:** Use campaign dashboard to submit directly
- **Collaborative Development:** Most approval processes involve ongoing GM consultation
- **Documentation:** Include background story and mechanical justifications

## GM Review Process

### Review Criteria
1. **Balance Assessment:** 
   - Power level appropriate for campaign
   - No exploitative combinations
   - Fair resource distribution

2. **Campaign Integration:**
   - Character fits setting and timeline
   - Background aligns with world lore
   - Motivations support group dynamics

3. **Mechanical Validation:**
   - Correct attribute calculations
   - Valid skill selections
   - Proper [[KRMA_System|KRMA]] expenditure
   - Age requirements satisfied

### GM Actions
**Approval Pathways:**
- **Direct Approval:** Character approved as submitted
- **Conditional Approval:** Minor adjustments required
- **Revision Request:** Significant changes needed
- **Rejection:** Character doesn't meet requirements

### Feedback Process
1. **GM Notification System:** Automated alerts for submitted characters
2. **Review Comments:** Specific feedback on required changes
3. **Back-and-Forth Refinement:** Iterative improvement process
4. **Final Authentication:** EŶ**∃**tehrNET validation once approved

## Age and Power Level Control

### KRMA Budget Requirements
- Seeds, Roots, and Branches all have [[KRMA_System|KRMA]] costs
- Higher power levels require more KRMA investment
- GM sets campaign starting KRMA budget limits
- Prevents overpowered starting characters
- **KV (Karma Value):** Individual component's value
- **TKV (Total Karmic Value):** Sum of all character components (inventory, skills, attributes, etc.)

### Age Accumulation
- Each Root and Branch adds age to the character
- Older characters generally have more capabilities through more Roots/Branches
- Age does NOT affect starting [[Health_Level_System|Health Level]] (determined by Seed)
- Natural limiting factor for character power (costs remaining lifespan)

## Integration Guidelines

### Collaborative Creation
Characters should be developed **with** the GM, not **for** the GM to approve. The approval process serves as final validation rather than first review.

### Campaign Alignment
- Characters must support the campaign's themes and goals
- Background should create adventure hooks and connections
- Group dynamic considerations important for party balance
