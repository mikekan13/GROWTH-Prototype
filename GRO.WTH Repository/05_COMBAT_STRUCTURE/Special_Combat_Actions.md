# Special Combat Actions

Beyond basic attacks and defenses, GROWTH provides several specialized combat maneuvers that can dramatically alter the flow of battle.

## Grappling

**Initiation:** Unarmed Combat skill check  
**Effect:** Restricts movement and weapon use for both participants  
**Resolution:** Opposed attribute checks to maintain or break grapple  

### Grappling Mechanics
- **Entry:** Successful Unarmed Combat roll to initiate
- **Maintenance:** Both parties make opposed checks each round
- **Restrictions:** Limited to small weapons and unarmed strikes
- **Movement:** Grappled characters cannot move freely
- **Escape:** Break free with successful opposed check

### Grappling Applications
- **Restraint:** Prevent enemy movement or weapon use
- **Positioning:** Control battlefield placement
- **Setup:** Enable coup de grace or called shots
- **Defense:** Prevent enemy from using large weapons

## Called Shots

**Difficulty:** +2 DR to target specific locations  
**Benefit:** Precise targeting of vulnerable areas  
**Risk:** Higher chance of missing completely  

### Called Shot Targets
- **Head:** Massive damage potential, knockout chance
- **Limbs:** Disable weapon use or movement
- **Joints:** Crippling injuries and mobility loss
- **Vital Organs:** Critical damage multipliers
- **Equipment:** Target worn armor or held items

### Called Shot Modifiers
- **Small Targets:** Additional DR increases
- **Moving Targets:** Further penalties apply
- **Range:** Distance increases difficulty
- **Conditions:** Lighting, weather, and cover affect accuracy

## Coup de Grace

**Target:** Helpless or unconscious opponents  
**Effect:** Instant kill or massive damage  
**Requirements:** Target must be unable to defend  

### Coup de Grace Conditions
- **Unconscious:** Target is knocked out or sleeping
- **Paralyzed:** Unable to move due to magic or injury
- **Helpless:** Bound, grappled, or otherwise restrained
- **Surprised:** Unaware of attacker's presence

### Coup de Grace Resolution
- **Automatic Hit:** No attack roll required against helpless target
- **Massive Damage:** Significantly increased damage potential
- **Instant Death:** May bypass normal health/frequency systems
- **Defensive Measures:** Some abilities may still protect target

## Advanced Combat Maneuvers

### Disarming
- **Skill Check:** Weapon skill vs opponent's weapon skill
- **Effect:** Remove weapon from opponent's grasp
- **Risk:** May damage or destroy weapon in process

### Sundering
- **Target:** Enemy's weapon or armor
- **Effect:** Damage or destroy equipment
- **Application:** Neutralize superior equipment advantages

### Combat Mobility
- **Movement Attacks:** Strike while moving past enemy
- **Evasive Actions:** Trade offense for defensive bonuses
- **Positioning:** Use terrain and obstacles tactically

## Integration with Core Combat

### Action Economy
Special actions may:
- **Replace:** Standard attack action
- **Modify:** Add effects to normal attacks  
- **Require:** Multiple actions or setup rounds

### Skill Requirements
Most special actions require:
- **Appropriate Skills:** Martial arts specializations
- **Attribute Minimums:** Physical capabilities
- **Training:** Some maneuvers need specific instruction
