# Combat Hit Locations

GROWTH uses **targeted hit locations** with specific **DR modifiers** and **condition effects** for realistic combat resolution.

## Hit Location Table

| Location | DR Modifier | Condition Effect | Coverage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Head & Neck** | +2 DR | Lose all offensive actions | ~10% |
| **Torso** | +0 DR | Lose one chosen action | ~40% |  
| **Arms** | -1 DR | Lose actions using that arm | ~25% |
| **Legs** | -1 DR | Movement penalties | ~25% |

## Hit Location Effects

### Head & Neck Hits
**DR Modifier:** +2 (harder to hit effectively)  
**Condition Effect:** Character loses **all offensive actions**  
**Duration:** Until medical treatment or healing  
**Severity:** Most debilitating hit location
**Lethality:** High chance of immediate incapacitation

### Torso Hits  
**DR Modifier:** +0 (standard target)  
**Condition Effect:** Character loses **one chosen action** per turn  
**Duration:** Until medical treatment or healing  
**Strategic Impact:** Reduces action economy significantly  
**Coverage:** Largest target area

### Arm Hits
**DR Modifier:** -1 (easier to hit)  
**Condition Effect:** Lose actions requiring use of **that specific arm**  
**Duration:** Until medical treatment or healing  
**Specific Effects:**
- Cannot use two-handed weapons
- Cannot perform actions requiring both hands
- Reduced climbing and grappling ability

### Leg Hits
**DR Modifier:** -1 (easier to hit)  
**Condition Effect:** **Movement penalties**  
**Duration:** Until medical treatment or healing  
**Specific Effects:**  
- Reduced movement speed
- Penalty to dodge attempts
- Difficulty with athletics requiring legs

## Targeted Attacks

### Aiming for Specific Locations
**Player Choice:** Can declare intention to target specific hit location  
**DR Adjustment:** Apply location's DR modifier to attack roll  
**Risk vs Reward:** Harder shots for more devastating effects

### Called Shots
**Head Shots:** Very difficult (+2 DR) but potentially incapacitating  
**Limb Shots:** Easier (-1 DR) but less immediately dangerous  
**Torso Shots:** Standard difficulty, reliable damage and effect

## Armor Coverage

**Full Coverage:** Armor protects all hit locations equally  
**Partial Coverage:** Some armor may not cover all locations  
**Location-Specific Armor:** Helmets, gauntlets, boots provide targeted protection

### Armor Gap Exploitation
**Targeting Gaps:** Skilled fighters can aim for unarmored areas  
**Additional Modifiers:** May increase DR further for precise targeting  
**Expert Techniques:** Higher skill levels enable better gap exploitation

## Medical Treatment

### Field Medicine
**Stabilization:** Prevent condition effects from worsening  
**Temporary Relief:** Reduce penalties until proper treatment  
**Skill Requirements:** Medical or healing skills needed

### Magical Healing
**Restoration Magic:** Can immediately resolve hit location conditions  
**Healing Spells:** Different spells may have varying effectiveness  
**Magical Medicine:** Combines mundane and magical treatment methods

## Lethal Hits

**Definition:** Any attack that bypasses the body layer entirely
**Defensive Actions:** Can use appropriate action (Body/Spirit/Soul) to defend against lethal hits if available
**Action Requirement:** Must have unused action matching the attack type to attempt defense
**Massive Damage:** Damage equal to 3x resist causes instant destruction
**Location Relevance:** Hit location affects lethality of attacks
