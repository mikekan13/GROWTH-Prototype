# Attribute Depletion Effects

When any attribute pool is reduced to **0 points**, the character suffers a specific depletion state until that attribute pool is restored above 0.

## Body Attributes

### Clout — Weak (0)
- **Effect:** Carry Level becomes 1 (minimal carrying capacity)
- **Impact:** Severely limits equipment and item carrying ability

### Celerity — Clumsy (0)  
- **Effect:** Before taking any action, pass DR 5 on Fate Die only (no Effort, no skills)
- **Failure:** Action Hesitates and GM imposes negative outcome
- **Impact:** Major action economy penalty and unpredictable failures

### Constitution — Exhausted (0)
- **Effect:** All ability point costs are doubled
- **Impact:** Effort becomes twice as expensive, severely limiting resource management

## Spirit Attributes

### Focus — Muted (0)
- **Effect:** Cannot add Effort to rolls
- **Unskilled Checks:** Skip wager step, roll Fate Die only
- **Skilled Checks:** Roll Skill Die, then Fate Die - no Effort allowed
- **Impact:** Major reduction in check reliability and success rates
### Frequency — Death's Door (0)
- **Effect:** Must roll vs. death with Lady Death intervention
- **Impact:** Character faces mortality mechanics and potential death
### Flow — Deafened (0)
- **Effect:** Cannot roll dice at all
- **Resolution:** All checks become **Effort-only vs DR** (no SD, no FD)
- **Step 0:** Declarations and locks still apply normally
- **Impact:** Extremely limiting - success requires heavy Effort expenditure

## Soul Attributes

### Willpower — Overwhelmed (0)
- **Effect:** Recovery suppressed
- **Short Rest:** Cannot take Short Rest (cannot spend Frequency for it)
- **Other Recovery:** All recovery effects restore half (round down, min 1 total)
- **Long Rest:** Still fully restores (unchanged)
### Wisdom — Confused (0)
- **Effect:** No color code hints and forced upfront wagering
- **Color Codes:** GM provides no difficulty hints (no Oracle assistance)
- **Check Flow:** Must wager Effort upfront even for skilled checks (like Unskilled flow)
- **Impact:** Removes two major advantages - information and informed decisions
### Wit — Incoherent (0)
- **Effect:** No Skills available
- **Resolution:** Must use **Unskilled** checks only
- **Impact:** Skill Die never applies, forces blind Effort wagering
- **Capability:** Raw attribute and Effort only



---
### Multiple Depletion Interactions
When multiple attributes are at 0, all applicable depletion effects stack. This can create extremely challenging scenarios requiring immediate attention to attribute recovery.
