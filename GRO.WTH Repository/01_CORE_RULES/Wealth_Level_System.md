# Wealth Level System

The **Wealth Level** system (the "W" in WTH - Wealth, Tech, Health) replaces traditional coin-counting with an **abstract resource management** approach, representing a character's overall financial status and purchasing power. Wealth establishes meta and campaign level ceilings for GROWTH.

## Wealth Level Scale (1-10)

### Level 1 - Destitute
**Financial Status:** Virtually no resources or possessions  
**Living Conditions:** Struggle to meet basic needs (food, shelter, clothing)  
**Income Sources:** Scavenging, begging, menial survival tasks  
**Equipment Access:** Limited to what can be found or crafted  
**Purchasing Power:** Cannot afford even basic items

### Level 2 - Impoverished  
**Financial Status:** Minimal resources, state of poverty  
**Living Conditions:** Can meet basic needs but lack stability/comfort  
**Income Sources:** Day labor, small odd jobs  
**Equipment Access:** Basic equipment, low-quality items  
**Purchasing Power:** Must carefully manage resources, prioritize essentials

### Level 3 - Poor
**Financial Status:** Basic living expenses covered, small disposable income  
**Living Conditions:** Modest lifestyle, some saving/scavenging for improvement  
**Income Sources:** Stable low-wage work, small quests  
**Equipment Access:** Slightly better equipment, occasional purchases  
**Purchasing Power:** Can afford low-cost items and services

### Level 4 - Modest
**Financial Status:** Simple but comfortable life  
**Living Conditions:** Basic luxuries, some financial security  
**Income Sources:** Stable job providing steady income  
**Equipment Access:** Standard equipment, can replace/repair as needed  
**Purchasing Power:** Small home ownership possible, modest lifestyle

### Level 5 - Comfortable  
**Financial Status:** Good quality resources and services  
**Living Conditions:** Comfortable lifestyle with leisure activities  
**Income Sources:** Good job with investment opportunities  
**Equipment Access:** Better equipment and services  
**Purchasing Power:** Decent home, connections for quests/business

### Level 6 - Affluent
**Financial Status:** Well-off with high standard of living  
**Living Conditions:** Significant luxuries, high-quality items  
**Income Sources:** Professional career, business ventures  
**Equipment Access:** High-quality equipment, can hire services  
**Purchasing Power:** Multiple properties, social/economic influence

### Level 7 - Wealthy
**Financial Status:** Very wealthy, life of luxury  
**Living Conditions:** Extensive luxuries, can afford most desires  
**Income Sources:** Major investments, business ownership  
**Equipment Access:** Best normally available equipment and services  
**Purchasing Power:** Significant influence, can leverage wealth for advantages

### Level 8 - Prosperous  
**Financial Status:** Extremely prosperous, vast resources  
**Living Conditions:** Opulent lifestyle, strong environmental influence  
**Income Sources:** Major enterprises, investment portfolios  
**Equipment Access:** Rare and unique equipment, large project financing  
**Purchasing Power:** Extensive contact networks, valuable information access

### Level 9 - Opulent
**Financial Status:** Immense wealth, extravagant luxury  
**Living Conditions:** Can affect significant environmental change  
**Income Sources:** Massive business empires, market influence  
**Equipment Access:** Custom items, skilled retinue employment  
**Purchasing Power:** High society influence, policy and event shaping

### Level 10 - Magnate
**Financial Status:** Pinnacle of wealth and influence  
**Living Conditions:** Controls vast resources, commanding presence  
**Income Sources:** Market manipulation, grand venture financing  
**Equipment Access:** Anything money can buy, considerable power wielding  
**Purchasing Power:** Can alter campaign course, shape world around them

## Wealth Check Mechanics

### Wealth Check System
**Each Wealth Level:** Provides **3 Wealth Checks** per level  
**Check Usage:** Represents purchasing power at that wealth tier  
**Recovery:** Wealth Checks typically recover over time or through income

### Purchase Resolution

#### Items Below Wealth Level
**Cost:** Free purchase (within reason)  
**Wealth Check:** No check required  
**Limitation:** GM discretion on "within reason" purchases  
**Example:** Wealthy (7) character buying extravagant meal "costs" nothing

#### Items At Wealth Level  
**Cost:** Uses 1 Wealth Check from current level  
**Wealth Check:** Reduces available checks by 1  
**Recovery:** Check may return with time/income  
**Example:** Comfortable (5) character buying Comfort-level item uses 1 check

#### Items Above Wealth Level
**Cost:** **Immediate Wealth Level decrease by 1**  
**Wealth Check:** Can purchase item 1 level above current wealth  
**Consequence:** Permanent wealth reduction until recovered  
**Example:** Modest (4) character buying Comfortable (5) item drops to Poor (3)

### Wealth Recovery

#### Natural Recovery
**Time-Based:** Wealth Checks recover through regular income  
**Career Integration:** Jobs and professions restore checks  
**Adventure Rewards:** Successful quests can restore or increase wealth  
**Investment Returns:** Business ventures provide check recovery

#### Wealth Level Advancement
**[[KRMA_System|KRMA]] Investment:** Can use KRMA to increase Wealth Level  
**[[Harvests_System|Harvest]] Rewards:** Major time investments can improve wealth  
**Adventure Success:** Significant achievements may increase wealth  
**Business Development:** Building enterprises increases wealth over time

## Integration with Other Systems

### Equipment and [[Material_System|Items]]
**Item Values:** All equipment has Wealth Level ratings for purchase  
**Availability:** Higher wealth items may be rare in lower wealth societies  
**Maintenance:** Expensive items may require ongoing wealth investment  
**Quality Correlation:** Higher wealth generally means better equipment quality

### [[Technology_Level_System|Technology Level]] Interaction
**Tech-Wealth Correlation:** Higher tech items typically require higher wealth  
**Cultural Availability:** Tech Level determines what wealth can purchase  
**Manufacturing Costs:** More advanced technology costs more wealth  
**Market Economics:** Tech Level advancement affects wealth economy

### Social and Campaign Effects
**Social Status:** Wealth Level affects NPC reactions and social opportunities  
**Adventure Access:** Some quests or information require minimum wealth levels  
**Lifestyle Maintenance:** Higher wealth levels may require ongoing expenses  
**Political Influence:** Wealth Level can provide campaign influence opportunities
