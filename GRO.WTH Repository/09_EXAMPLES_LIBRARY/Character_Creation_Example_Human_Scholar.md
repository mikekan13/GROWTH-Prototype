# Character Creation Example: Human Scholar

This example demonstrates creating a scholarly character focused on knowledge, magic theory, and social connections.

## Step 1: Character Concept and Backstory

**Character Concept:** Lysander Quill, a middle-aged human scholar who spent decades researching ancient magical texts in the Great Library before circumstances forced him to leave his comfortable academic life.

**Background Story:** Born to merchant parents, Lysander showed early aptitude for learning and was sponsored to attend the Academy. He spent 20 years as a researcher and librarian, becoming an expert in magical theory despite having only modest practical casting ability. Recent political upheaval forced him to flee with stolen research documents.

## Step 2: Seed Selection

**Chosen Seed:** Human - Standard Template
- **Starting Frequency Budget:** 100 points
- **Base Fate Die:** d6 (reliable human baseline)
- **Natural [[Health_Level_System|Health Level]]:** 5 (~150 year natural lifespan) 
- **Inherent Abilities:** None (balanced with higher Frequency)
- **Attribute Baselines:** All attributes start at 8

## Step 3: Custom Root Creation (GM)

**Root Name:** "Academy Scholar"
**Frequency Cost:** 60 points (reduced by age)
**Age Added:** 15 years (reduces cost by 30 points, final cost: 30)

**Root Effects:**
- **Skills Gained:**
  - Research: Level 8 (d8 die)
  - Ancient Languages: Level 6 (d6 die) 
  - Magic Theory: Level 7 (d6 die)
  - Literacy: Level 10 (d8 die)
  - Social Etiquette: Level 4 (d4 die)
- **Attribute Modifications:**
  - Wit +4 (brilliant analytical mind)
  - Wisdom +2 (scholarly insight)
  - Constitution -2 (sedentary lifestyle)
- **Starting Wealth:** Level 6 (Affluent - professor's salary)
- **Technology Level:** 4 (Renaissance era knowledge)
- **Social Connections:** Academic network, library access

## Step 4: Custom Branch Creation (GM)

**Branch Name:** "Political Exile" 
**Frequency Cost:** 40 points (reduced by age)
**Age Added:** 10 years (reduces cost by 20 points, final cost: 20)

**Branch Effects:**
- **Skills Gained:**
  - Stealth: Level 3 (flat +3 bonus)
  - Survival: Level 2 (flat +2 bonus)
  - Diplomacy: Level 5 (d4 die)
  - Information Networks: Level 4 (d4 die)
- **Equipment:** 
  - Stolen research documents (valuable but dangerous)
  - Scholar's travel pack
  - Concealed blade
  - Academic credentials (forged)
- **Thorns:** "Wanted by Authorities" (political enemies)
- **Nectars:** "Rare Knowledge" (possesses unique information)

## Step 5: Final Character Build

### Age Calculation
- **Base Age:** 25 (young adult human)
- **Root Age Added:** 15 years  
- **Branch Age Added:** 10 years
- **Final Age:** 50 years old

### Frequency Budget Management
- **Starting Frequency:** 100 points
- **Root Cost:** 30 points (after age reduction)
- **Branch Cost:** 20 points (after age reduction)  
- **Remaining Frequency:** 50 points (retained for play)

### Final Attributes
| Attribute | Base | Root | Branch | Total |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Clout** | 8 | 0 | 0 | 8 |
| **Celerity** | 8 | 0 | 0 | 8 |  
| **Constitution** | 8 | -2 | 0 | 6 |
| **Flow** | 8 | 0 | 0 | 8 |
| **Frequency** | 8 | 0 | 0 | 8 (+50 from budget) |
| **Focus** | 8 | 0 | 0 | 8 |
| **Willpower** | 8 | 0 | 0 | 8 |
| **Wisdom** | 8 | +2 | 0 | 10 |
| **Wit** | 8 | +4 | 0 | 12 |

### Skills Summary
- **Research:** Level 8 (d8) - Expert researcher
- **Literacy:** Level 10 (d8) - Master of written word
- **Magic Theory:** Level 7 (d6) - Theoretical knowledge  
- **Ancient Languages:** Level 6 (d6) - Linguistic scholar
- **Diplomacy:** Level 5 (d4) - Social training
- **Social Etiquette:** Level 4 (d4) - Court manners
- **Information Networks:** Level 4 (d4) - Academic contacts
- **Stealth:** Level 3 (+3 flat) - Learned from exile
- **Survival:** Level 2 (+2 flat) - Basic outdoor skills

### Character Traits
**Nectars:**
- "Rare Knowledge" - Access to unique research and ancient lore
- "Academic Network" - Connections throughout scholarly community

**Thorns:**  
- "Wanted by Authorities" - Political enemies seek his capture
- "Physically Frail" - Low Constitution affects endurance

**Goals (GRO.vines):**
- Find safe haven to continue research
- Expose the corruption that forced his exile
- Discover the truth behind the ancient texts he stole
- *Available 4th GRO.vine slot (Human "Ambitious" nectar provides +1 capacity)*

**Fears:**
- Capture by political enemies
- Loss of his research and life's work  
- Being wrong about his theories

## Step 6: Starting Equipment and Wealth

**Wealth Level 6 (Affluent):** 18 Wealth Checks available (6 levels × 3 checks)

**Starting Equipment:**
- **Research Materials:** Ancient text fragments, notes, maps
- **Scholar's Tools:** Writing supplies, magnifying glass, reference books
- **Travel Gear:** Quality travel clothes, sturdy pack, bedroll
- **Weapons:** Concealed dagger, walking stick (club)
- **Valuables:** Academic credentials, letter of credit, gemstone (emergency funds)

## Step 7: Magic and Mana

**Mana Calculation:** 
- **Soul Mana:** KV 170 ÷ 10 = 17 points
- **Body Mana:** Constitution 6 = 6 points  
- **Spirit Mana:** (Willpower + Wisdom + Wit) ÷ 3 = (8+10+12) ÷ 3 = 10 points

**Known Spells:** 
- **Light** (Alteration/Mercy) - Theoretical knowledge, basic practice
- **Detect Magic** (Divination/Balance) - Academic research tool
- **Comprehend Languages** (Enchantment/Mercy) - Scholarly necessity

## Gameplay Notes

### Character Strengths
- **Research Master:** Best-in-class information gathering
- **Social Connections:** Academic network provides resources
- **Magical Theory:** Understands magic even if not powerful caster
- **Languages:** Can read ancient texts and communicate broadly

### Character Weaknesses  
- **Combat Ineffective:** Low physical attributes, minimal combat training
- **Political Liability:** Wanted status creates constant danger
- **Resource Dependent:** Needs books, libraries, civilization to thrive
- **Age Effects:** 50 years old, approaching middle age decline

### Adventure Hooks
- **Research Quests:** Ancient mysteries need investigation
- **Political Intrigue:** Exile status creates complex social situations
- **Knowledge Broker:** Unique information creates opportunities and dangers
- **Mentor Role:** Can teach and guide other characters

### Potential Character Development
- **Combat Training:** Could learn basic self-defense skills
- **Magic Practice:** Theory knowledge could develop into practical ability  
- **Network Expansion:** Build new connections outside academic circles
- **Redemption Arc:** Clear name and return to scholarly life
