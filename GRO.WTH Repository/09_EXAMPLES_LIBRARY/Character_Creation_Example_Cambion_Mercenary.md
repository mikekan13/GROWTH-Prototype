# Character Creation Example: Cambion Mercenary

This example demonstrates the complete [[Character_Approval_Process|character creation process]] using the **Seeds, Roots, and Branches** system with collaborative GM-player development.

## Character Concept

**Player Vision:** A half-demon, half-human warrior with a military background who became a professional mercenary.

## Step 1: Seed Selection

**Player:** "I'd like to create a character who's a half-demon, half-human warrior."

**GM:** "Perfect! Let's use the **Cambion Seed** for your half-demon heritage."

### Cambion Seed Details
- **Species:** Cambion (Half-Demon)
- **Starting Fate Die:** d8
- **Starting Frequency:** 5
- **Life Span Level:** 4
- **Physical Traits:** Fiery eyes, dark aura, slight horns
- **Base Body Resists:** Fire resistance

#### Attributes (Cambion Seed)
- **Clout:** 3
- **Celerity:** 2
- **Constitution:** 4
- **Focus:** 6
- **Flow:** 3
- **Willpower:** 5
- **Wisdom:** 3
- **Wit:** 2

#### Starting Features
- **Augments:** Minor Fire Resistance
- **Starting Skills:** Dark Magic (Level 3), Intimidation (Level 2)
- **Nectars:** Demonic Pact (Enhances magical abilities)
- **Thorns:** Vulnerability to Holy Attacks

**Player:** "Yes, that sounds great!"

## Step 2: Root Selection

**GM:** "Excellent. Now let's explore your character's upbringing. What was her childhood like?"

**Player:** "She grew up in a harsh, war-torn environment, which made her tough and resilient."

**GM:** "I have the perfect Root: **Warrior's Childhood**. This reflects growing up amid conflict and hardship."

### Warrior's Childhood Root
- **Background:** Harsh, war-torn environment fostering toughness and resilience
- **Skills:** Survival (Level 3), Weapon Training (Level 2)
- **Attributes:** +3 Constitution, +2 Clout
- **Theme:** Military upbringing, conflict exposure, survival training

#### Updated Attributes (After Root)
- **Clout:** 5 (3 + 2)
- **Celerity:** 2
- **Constitution:** 7 (4 + 3)
- **Focus:** 6
- **Flow:** 3
- **Willpower:** 5
- **Wisdom:** 3
- **Wit:** 2

**Player:** "Perfect! That fits her backstory exactly."

## Step 3: Branch Selection

**GM:** "Great! Now for Branches - what experiences and training has she acquired in adulthood?"

**Player:** "She spent years as a mercenary, honing her combat skills and learning to survive in various environments."

**GM:** "The **Mercenary Branch** is ideal for this background."

### Mercenary Branch
- **Background:** Years of professional combat as hired warrior
- **Skills:** Tactics (Level 2), Hand-to-Hand Combat (Level 3)
- **Attributes:** +2 Clout, +2 Constitution
- **Theme:** Professional warfare, tactical expertise, combat mastery

#### Final Attributes (After Branch)
- **Clout:** 7 (5 + 2)
- **Celerity:** 2
- **Constitution:** 9 (7 + 2)
- **Focus:** 6
- **Flow:** 3
- **Willpower:** 5
- **Wisdom:** 3
- **Wit:** 2

**Player:** "Yes, that sounds perfect!"

## Final Character Summary

### Core Identity
**Name:** [Player Choice]  
**Species:** Cambion (Half-Demon)  
**Background:** War-torn childhood → Professional mercenary  
**Age:** [Seed + Root + Branch age accumulation]

### Final Statistics
**Fate Die:** d8  
**Frequency:** 5  
**Life Span Level:** 4

#### Complete Skill List
- **Dark Magic:** Level 3 (Seed)
- **Intimidation:** Level 2 (Seed)
- **Survival:** Level 3 (Root)
- **Weapon Training:** Level 2 (Root)
- **Tactics:** Level 2 (Branch)
- **Hand-to-Hand Combat:** Level 3 (Branch)

#### Special Features
**Nectars:** Demonic Pact (Magical enhancement)  
**Thorns:** Vulnerability to Holy Attacks  
**Augments:** Minor Fire Resistance

### Character Strengths
**Physical Combat:** High Clout and Constitution make her formidable in melee  
**Tactical Knowledge:** Tactics skill provides battlefield awareness  
**Survival Expertise:** Can handle harsh environments and dangerous situations  
**Dark Magic:** Supernatural abilities complement combat prowess  
**Intimidating Presence:** Natural demon heritage aids in social control

### Character Challenges
**Holy Vulnerability:** Significant weakness to divine/holy attacks  
**Limited Mental Attributes:** Lower Wisdom and Wit create knowledge gaps  
**Social Complications:** Demonic heritage may cause trust issues  
**Moral Complexity:** Demonic nature vs human conscience creates internal conflict

## GM Notes

### Campaign Integration
**Adventure Hooks:** Military contacts, mercenary reputation, demonic heritage complications  
**Party Role:** Tank/warrior with tactical support capabilities  
**Character Arc Potential:** Redemption, embracing/rejecting demonic nature, leadership development

### Balance Assessment
**Power Level:** Appropriate for starting campaign with strong combat focus  
**Weaknesses:** Clear vulnerabilities prevent overpowering  
**Growth Potential:** Room for development in mental attributes and new skills

## Final Approval

**GM:** "Your character sheet reflects all these choices perfectly. Let's create a background story that ties everything together, then we'll submit for final approval through the GROWTH software."
