# Three Pillars of Magic

The GROWTH magic system is organized around **three fundamental pillars** that govern all magical practice: **Mercy**, **Severity**, and **Balance**.

## Pillar of Mercy
*Governed by [[Three_Pillar_Attributes|Flow]] attribute*

**Philosophy:** Receiving, acceptance, divine inspiration  
**Approach:** Passive magical connection, working with natural forces  
**Schools:**
- **[[Enchantment]]** - Mind control
- **[[Restoration]]** - Healing and growth
- **[[Fortune]]** - Luck manipulation and augmentation

## Pillar of Severity  
*Governed by [[Three_Pillar_Attributes|Focus]] attribute*

**Philosophy:** Giving, manifestation, divine will  
**Approach:** Active magical projection, imposing will on reality  
**Schools:**
- **[[Force]]** - Energy manipulation
- **[[Alteration]]** - Matter manipulation  
- **[[Conjuration]]** - Summoning and portals

## Pillar of Balance
*Uses combinations of attributes*

**Philosophy:** Harmony between opposing forces  
**Approach:** Synthesis of Mercy and Severity principles  
**Schools:**
- **[[Illusion]]** - Reality deception
- **[[Dissolution]]** - Life and death magic
- **[[Abjuration]]** - Defensive magic  
- **[[Divination]]** - Mind reading, time manipulation

## Pillar Interactions

### Complementary Forces
**Mercy + Severity = Balance:** Most powerful magic combines opposing forces  
**Pure Pillar Magic:** Easier to learn, more predictable  
**Mixed Pillar Magic:** More complex but potentially more powerful

### Attribute Synergy
**Flow-Based Magic:** Intuitive, receptive, healing-focused  
**Focus-Based Magic:** Willful, projective, force-oriented  
**Balanced Magic:** Requires understanding of both approaches

## Casting Methodologies

### Spell Weaving (Conscious Practice)
- Uses appropriate magic school skills
- Combines with other skills for synergy effects
- No [[Monkey_Paw]] effects on success
- More predictable and reliable

### Wild Casting (Raw Power)
- Direct application of magical force
- Faster but more dangerous
- Always carries [[Monkey_Paw]] risks
- Power governed by attribute levels

## Magic Resolution Mechanics

**Governor System:** Each magical effect specifies target attributes that both the caster and defender can use. The caster uses these attributes to fuel their magic, while the defender can use the same attributes to resist or reduce the effect.

**Target Attributes:** Magic spells and abilities specify which attributes they target, determining what pools both sides can draw from during resolution.
