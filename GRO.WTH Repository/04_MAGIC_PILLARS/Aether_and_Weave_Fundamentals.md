# Aether & Weave Fundamentals

The metaphysical foundation of GROWTH magic rests on understanding **the Aether**, **Mana**, and the **Weave** that connects all existence.

## The Aether
**Definition:** Veins of existence connecting all universes  
**Function:** Network through which magical energy flows  
**Nature:** Transcends individual realities and dimensions  
**Access:** Consciousness can tap into Aetheric flows

### Aetheric Properties
- **Universal Connection:** Links all planes of existence
- **Energy Conduit:** Carries raw magical potential
- **Information Network:** Allows divination and communication across vast distances
- **Reality Interface:** Where will interacts with physical law

## Mana System
**Mana:** Raw material distilled through Aether connection  
**Source:** Personal connection to Aetheric flows  
**Function:** Fuel for magical effects

### Mana Generation
Characters generate mana through:
- **Meditation:** Conscious connection to Aether
- **Natural Recovery:** Passive regeneration during rest  
- **Ritual Practice:** Focused magical exercises
- **Environmental Sources:** Places of power, ley lines

### Mana Conversion Rates

| Source | Mana Yield | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Spirit Connection | KV/10 (rounded up) | Primary personal source |
| Body Part Sacrifice | 6 mana | Permanent physical cost |
| Life Force | KV/100 (rounded up) | Extremely dangerous |
| Environmental | Variable | Location dependent |

## The Weave
**Definition:** The structured pattern through which mana becomes magical effect  
**Function:** Translates will and energy into reality alteration  
**Mastery:** Understanding Weave patterns enables [[Spell_Weaving]]

### Weave Interactions
- **Spell Weaving:** Conscious manipulation of Weave patterns
- **Wild Casting:** Forcing mana through Weave without proper understanding
- **Weave Disruption:** Anti-magic effects that damage Weave structure
- **Weave Synergy:** Multiple casters working together

## Magical Catalysts
**Purpose:** Items that enhance or focus magical energy  
**Function:** Reduce mana costs, improve spell accuracy  
**Types:** Focuses, components, ritual implements

### Catalyst Categories
**Prima Materias:** Fundamental magical substances (Level 1-8 power)  
**Elemental Focuses:** Aligned with specific magical schools  
**Personal Catalysts:** Items attuned to individual casters  
**Ritual Components:** Consumed in spell casting

## Prima Materias System
