# Magic School: Force

**Pillar:** [[Three_Pillars_Overview|Severity]]  
**Governing Attribute:** [[Three_Pillar_Attributes|Focus]]  
**Description:** Power to manipulate energy, usually to cause direct damage or movement  
**Resistance:** Resisted with Nimbleness

## Prima Materia Connection

**Associated Prima:** Iron Prima (Force)

## Casting Methods

### Spell Weaving
- Uses Force magic skill
- Governed by Focus attribute
- Avoids [[Monkey_Paw_System|Monkey Paw]] effects on success
- More predictable and controlled

### Wild Casting  
- Direct Focus attribute usage
- Faster but carries [[Monkey_Paw_System|Monkey Paw]] risks
- Power governed by Focus attribute level

## Mana Usage

**Enhancement:** Can be enhanced with [[Mana_System|mana points]] for increased casting power
**Focus Integration:** Focus attribute provides effort for spell casting

## Force Applications

**Direct Damage:** Various damage types (Physical, Fire, Cold, Lightning, Acid)
**Object Manipulation:** Moving, crushing, and manipulating physical objects
**Energy Projection:** Pure force effects and kinetic manipulation

## Spell Strength

Uses the standard [[Spell_Strength_Levels|1-10 strength scale]] for determining:
- Amount of damage or force generated
- Size of objects that can be manipulated
- Resistance against dispelling
- Mana costs required
