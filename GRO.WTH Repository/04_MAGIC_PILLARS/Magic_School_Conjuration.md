# Magic School: Conjuration

**Pillar:** [[Three_Pillars_Overview|Severity]]  
**Governing Attribute:** [[Three_Pillar_Attributes|Focus]]  
**Description:** Powers to summon beings or objects and create portals  
**Resistance:** When directed at creatures, resisted with Conviction

## Prima Materia Connection

**Associated Prima:** Mercury Prima (Conjuration)

## Casting Methods

### Spell Weaving
- Uses Conjuration magic skill
- Governed by Focus attribute
- Avoids [[Monkey_Paw_System|Monkey Paw]] effects on success
- More predictable and controlled

### Wild Casting  
- Direct Focus attribute usage
- Faster but carries [[Monkey_Paw_System|Monkey Paw]] risks
- Power governed by Focus attribute level

## Mana Usage

**Enhancement:** Can be enhanced with [[Mana_System|mana points]] for increased casting power
**Focus Integration:** Focus attribute provides effort for spell casting

## Conjuration Applications

**Summoning:** Bringing objects or creatures from other locations
**Transportation:** Teleportation and portal creation
**Dimensional Travel:** Required for interplanetary, intergalactic, interdimensional, and interplanar effects

## Spell Strength

Uses the standard [[Spell_Strength_Levels|1-10 strength scale]] for determining:
- Size and power of summoned entities
- Range and duration of portals
- Resistance against dispelling
- Mana costs required
