# Magic School: Alteration

**Pillar:** [[Three_Pillars_Overview|Severity]]  
**Governing Attribute:** [[Three_Pillar_Attributes|Focus]]  
**Description:** Manipulation of matter; changing things physically  
**Resistance:** Resisted with Toughness

## Prima Materia Connection

**Associated Prima:** Uranium Prima (Alteration)

## Casting Methods

Magic skills are SPIRIT-pillared. The Alteration skill itself is what is rolled — the governing Focus attribute defines the school's pillar tag but does NOT replace the skill in the casting roll.

### Wild Casting
- **Roll:** `Fate Die + Alteration School Skill die vs DR`
- **No Effort wager allowed** — wild is unwagered.
- **Failure** triggers a [[Monkey_Paw_System|Monkey Paw]] AND marks Alteration trainable for the next rest.
- Faster, riskier, but the only casting method that grants skill-growth opportunity.

### Woven Spells
- **Roll:** `Fate Die + Alteration School Skill die + Associated non-magic Skill die vs DR`
- **No Monkey Paw** on failure.
- **No trainable marker** on failure (this is the safety/learning tradeoff).
- More predictable; safer, but no skill-growth opportunity from this cast.

### Multi-School Spells
When a spell involves Alteration and one or more other schools, use the **WEAKEST** of the involved School skills as the School die.

## Trainable Marker

Wild Casting failures mark THIS School as trainable. On next rest, the caster may Spend 1 Frequency to gain +1 to this School's skill level. Woven Spell failures do NOT mark the School trainable (the safety/learning tradeoff).

## Mana Usage

**Mana pool:** Mana is stored on catalysts or in body parts, separate from any attribute pool.  
**Bonus:** Each mana point spent adds **+1** to the casting roll.  
**Channel capacity per cast:** Equal to **Overall Spellcasting Level** = sum of all 10 School levels.

## Spell Strength

Uses the standard [[Spell_Strength_Levels|1-10 strength scale]] for determining:
- Complexity of transformation possible
- Duration of alterations
- Resistance against dispelling
- Mana costs required
