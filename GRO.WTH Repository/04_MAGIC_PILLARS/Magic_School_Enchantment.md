# Magic School: Enchantment

**Pillar:** [[Three_Pillars_Overview|Mercy]]  
**Governing Attribute:** [[Three_Pillar_Attributes|Flow]]  
**Description:** The power over minds  
**Resistance:** Resisted with creature's Ingenuity

## Prima Materia Connection

**Associated Prima:** Copper Prima (Enchantment)

## Casting Methods

### Spell Weaving
- Uses Enchantment magic skill
- Governed by Flow attribute
- Avoids [[Monkey_Paw_System|Monkey Paw]] effects on success
- More predictable and controlled

### Wild Casting  
- Direct Flow attribute usage
- Faster but carries [[Monkey_Paw_System|Monkey Paw]] risks
- Power governed by Flow attribute level

## Mana Usage

**Enhancement:** Can be enhanced with [[Mana_System|mana points]] for increased casting power
**Flow Integration:** Flow attribute provides effort for spell casting

## Control Types

**Suggestible:** Light mental influence, resisted with Ingenuity
**Slave:** Direct mental control, resisted with Charisma  
**Complete Control:** Total domination, resisted with both Charisma and Ingenuity

## Spell Strength

Uses the standard [[Spell_Strength_Levels|1-10 strength scale]] for determining:
- Depth of mental control possible
- Duration of enchantments
- Resistance against dispelling
- Mana costs required
