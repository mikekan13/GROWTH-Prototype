# Magic School: Dissolution

**Pillar:** [[Three_Pillars_Overview|Balance]]  
**Governing Attribute:** [[Three_Pillar_Attributes|Flow]] AND [[Three_Pillar_Attributes|Focus]]  
**Description:** Power over life and death  
**Resistance:** Resisted with Virility

## Prima Materia Connection

**Associated Prima:** Plutonium Prima (Dissolution)

## Casting Methods

### Spell Weaving
- Uses Dissolution magic skill
- Governed by Flow AND Focus attributes (can use either or both for effort)
- Avoids [[Monkey_Paw_System|Monkey Paw]] effects on success
- More predictable and controlled

### Wild Casting  
- Direct Flow and/or Focus attribute usage
- Faster but carries [[Monkey_Paw_System|Monkey Paw]] risks
- Power governed by attribute levels

## Mana Usage

**Enhancement:** Can be enhanced with [[Mana_System|mana points]] for increased casting power
**Dual Integration:** Both Flow and Focus attributes can provide effort for spell casting

## Dissolution Applications

**Death Magic:** Kill creatures and destroy souls
**Life Manipulation:** Increase or decrease lifespan
**Soul Magic:** Create souls, steal mana, tether souls to bodies
**Mana Theft:** Drain magical energy from other beings

## Special Considerations

**Extreme Power:** Death and soul magic represents some of the most powerful effects in GROWTH
**High Resistance:** Most dissolution effects allow strong saving throws
**Ethical Implications:** Use of dissolution magic often carries moral consequences

## Spell Strength

Uses the standard [[Spell_Strength_Levels|1-10 strength scale]] for determining:
- Lethality and soul-affecting power
- Lifespan manipulation magnitude
- Resistance against dispelling
- Mana costs required
