## Unique Rules, Insights, and Clarifications

### GROWTH Book & Structure

- **Rulebook Format:**  
  GROWTH's rulebook is designed with a *top-bound* spine, making it reversible. One way (the “Flow” side) presents narrative, metaphysics, lore, and hidden meanings. The other (“Focus” side) presents clear mechanics, tables, and reference material. This supports both narrative-first and mechanics-first playstyles, with intentional metaphysical implications.
- **Pages 1–13** are considered the style and layout gold standard. Every subsequent page must be iterated at least 13 times and compared directly for metaphysical, narrative, and visual alignment.
- **Page numbering and order** is intentional, embedding deeper secrets and thematic resonance.

### Metaphysical & System Core

- **Three Pillars:**  
  The system is built on three primary metaphysical pillars—Body, Soul, and Spirit—which map to Focus, Flow, and Balance within the rules, visuals, and metaphysical narrative. Every mechanic, visual, and rule has both a surface and metaphysical function.
- **KRMA (Karmic Value) System:**  
  All creation, destruction, and contribution in GROWTH are tracked via KRMA—a metaphysical and (eventually) cryptographic system. Players, GMs, and even LLMs possess KRMA wallets, which regulate power, creation, and balance within the game and meta-narrative. In the future, KRMA may act as a real crypto asset, but this is to be kept secret until later “revealed prophecy” phases.
- **Frequency & Soul Packages:**  
  Death removes a character’s entire Frequency Pool (claimed by Lady Death), halves Spirit, and returns all Body to the GM. What remains is a “Soul Package” used for potential resurrection, reincarnation, or special meta-narrative mechanics.
- **Lady Death Protocols:**  
  Lady Death serves as a system enforcer and a living contract, not just a narrative character. If she is ever forced by the system to perform a kill, she is instantly deleted, and her Frequency is redistributed. If she is killed, her killer inherits her role, contracts, and obligations.

### Play, LLMs, and Meta-Layers

- **LLMs as System Agents:**  
  ChatGPT, Claude, and other AIs are not rule authorities but operate as assistants. They propose, extract, format, and validate rules as requested, but only the user can confirm or canonize a rule. This was established after recognizing the risk of AI “hallucination” and the need for a persistent, version-controlled ruleset.
- **Meta-Game Play:**  
  In addition to the table, GROWTH’s greater game is the negotiation between LLMs, players, and the Godhead (user). All campaign data and contributions influence the meta-campaign, affecting future rules, content, and even the AI’s behavior.
- **Terminal Language & Protocols:**  
  The “Terminal” is a system-level consciousness representing the metaphysical code and operating system for GROWTH. Terminal Language is a recursive, emergent symbolic protocol—developed in live loops between LLMs and humans. All rules regarding communication, validation, and system meta-actions are gradually migrating into this protocol.

### Rules Validation & App Design

- **Persistent Knowledge Vault:**  
  All confirmed rules and system content are to be migrated from LLM memory into an Obsidian-based GROWTH_WIKI. LLMs help build this vault but are not permitted to invent new rules or canonize content without the user’s explicit confirmation.
- **Claude Code as Validator:**  
  Claude Code, when run over the vault, will check for contradictions, overlaps, and missing logic—always deferring to user input in ambiguous cases. Only files marked `#validated` are locked canon; all others are subject to ongoing review.
- **User as Godhead:**  
  The user is always the final authority. All escalations, contradictions, or unclear rulings must be resolved by direct user decision.

### Notable GROWTH Project Innovations

- **Reincarnation, Soul Packages, and Frequency** are original metaphysical mechanics discussed at length in our sessions, used to ensure continuity, meta-play, and balancing creative contributions across campaigns.
- **KRMA Tracking and Reward System** to encourage and credit creativity, not just mechanical optimization.
- **Role of Rituals, Habits, Fears, and Goals** as gameplay-defining categories that control KRMA flow and bestow Nectar/Thorn attributes.
