**Complete Attribute Pool Depletion Effects:**

**BODY**

- **Clout — Weak (0):** Carry Level becomes 1
- **Celerity — Clumsy (0):** Before taking an action, pass DR 5 on a Fate Die (no Effort, no skills). Fail = you Hesitate and the GM imposes a negative outcome
- **Constitution — Exhausted (0):** All ability point costs are doubled

**SOUL**

- **Focus — Muted (0):** You cannot add Effort to rolls
- **Frequency — Death's Door (0):** You must roll vs. death with Lady Death
- **Flow — Deafened (0):** You cannot roll dice; you can only spend Effort to meet DRs

**SPIRIT**

- **Willpower — Overwhelmed (0):** Recovery suppressed. You cannot take a Short Rest (and can't spend Frequency for it). Other recovery effects restore half to you (round down, min 1 total); Long Rest still fully restores
- **Wit — Incoherent (0):** No Skills. You can't use skills for checks—raw attribute (and Effort, if available) only
- **Wisdom — Confused (0):** No color code hints and you must wager effort upfront even for skilled checks (like unskilled flow)

**All nine depletion effects now defined!** Each hits a different core mechanic in a meaningful way.
